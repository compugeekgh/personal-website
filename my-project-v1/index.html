About Me

I'm Theophilus Bosomtwe, a Ghanaian by birth and a technology enthusiast deeply passionate about leveraging the power of code to create impactful solutions. Based in Burkina Faso, I identify as a self-taught Full Stack Web Developer specializing in the MERN stack (MongoDB, Express.js, React.js, Node.js), with additional expertise in Python.

My journey began with a fascination for technology and its potential to shape the world around us. This interest blossomed into a dedicated pursuit of learning and mastering web development skills. As a Full Stack Developer, I find immense satisfaction in building robust and engaging applications from conception to deployment.

Currently, I'm the CEO of Jnana Technology, an entrepreneurial venture that allows me to channel my skills and passion into building innovative tech solutions for businesses and individuals alike.

This self-employment journey has been both challenging and rewarding, teaching me the importance of adaptability, resilience, and a relentless drive to succeed.

My commitment to continuous learning ensures I stay ahead of the curve in this rapidly evolving field. I believe that technology is a powerful tool for progress and social impact, and I'm dedicated to using my skills to make a positive difference.
