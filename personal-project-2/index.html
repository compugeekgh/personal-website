About Me

Hello! I'm Theophilus Bosomtwe, a Ghanaian currently living in Burkina Faso. My journey has been anything but conventional — I spent over 10 years in the construction industry, where I specialized in site supervision, project management, and survey work. While I enjoyed my time in construction, I’ve always had a deep fascination with technology, which led me to make a big shift in my career.

Background

Today, I’m a self-taught developer, passionate about full-stack web development, IT support, and problem-solving. My skills range from HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to Python, and I’m committed to continuously learning and growing in the tech field. I may not have a formal education in tech, but I’ve spent countless hours teaching myself and putting my skills into practice through personal projects.

Passion

When I’m not coding, you’ll find me diving into tech-related content, reading about the latest industry trends, or brainstorming new startup ideas. I’m also a parent who’s dedicated to creating a better future for my family by embracing new opportunities and challenges.

Philosophy

I believe in continuous learning and improvement. The tech landscape is ever-evolving, and I strive to stay up-to-date with the latest trends and best practices.
